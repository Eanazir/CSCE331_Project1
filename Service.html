Feed My Starving Children (FMSC)


Millions of children die each year from avoidable conditions like pneumonia, diarrhea, malaria, and malnutrition. Nearly 50% of mortality in children under 5 are still related to hunger. That is why I try to make it a habit to volunteer at Feed My Starving Children (FMSC) . FMSC holds that food is the foundation of hope. The mission of FMSC is to see every child healed in body and spirit. FMSC works with food distribution partners who commit to a long-term relationship with communities, enabling them to transition from relief to development. During almost 2 hours we packed 90 boxes that would feed 53 kids for a year!

Save the Turtles!


A cause that I am very passionate about is saving Turtles, specifically Sea turtles. The health and future of all life on earth are correlated with the health of all animals, such as sea turtles. To change people's perceptions, attitudes, and behaviors towards biodiversity, it is essential to raise awareness of the delicate balance between life in the oceans and on land. People have a duty to speak up for sea turtles and other marine creatures that are seriously endangered due to global commercial, cultural, and personal practices. That is why I have been in support of the Turtle Island Restoration Network (TIRN) . For 30 years, Turtle Island Restoration Network (TIRN) has been a leading advocate for the world's oceans and marine wildlife. I would like to go to one of their programs in the future to volunteer with Sea Turtles.
